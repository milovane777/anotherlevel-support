Privacy Policy

healthy eye · Last updated: July 17, 2026

healthy eye (“the app”), developed by another_level_studio (“we”), respects your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

We do not collect your data

healthy eye does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. There is no account system and no advertising or analytics service that sends data off your device. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

On-device storage

Your activity — eye-care checklists, exercise records, Eye Age results, notes, and settings — is stored only on your device using the operating system’s local storage. Deleting the app removes this data. Optional in-app usage statistics are stored locally and can be turned off or cleared in Settings; they are never sent anywhere.

Permissions

  • Microphone & Speech Recognition (optional): used only for the voice-based vision self-check. Audio is processed by Apple’s speech recognition to turn your spoken answers into text in real time. We do not store or receive your audio or transcripts.
  • Notifications (optional): used to deliver the local 20-20-20 eye-break reminders you schedule. They are generated on your device.
  • Sharing: when you choose to share a result, the content is handed to the app you select via the system share sheet. We do not receive that content.

Third parties

The app does not include advertising networks, third-party analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Children’s privacy

Because the app collects no personal data, it does not knowingly collect information from children.

Medical disclaimer

healthy eye is a general wellness and educational app. Eye Age, scores, and self-checks are general, non-clinical estimates and are not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. For any concerns about your eyes, please consult an eye-care professional.

Changes

We may update this policy; the “Last updated” date above will reflect any changes.
